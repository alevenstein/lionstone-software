## Overview

*Waystone* is a notebook app for people who already write in Markdown and want their phone to keep up. Open, type, and the cursor is already in place.

## Highlights

- **Offline-first.** Sync is optional and end-to-end encrypted.
- **Gesture-driven.** Swipe between notes, long-press to pin, two-finger tap to undo.
- **Plain files.** Notes are just `.md` on disk — exportable in one tap.

## Status

In active development. Targeting an Android-first launch; an iOS port is under consideration.
