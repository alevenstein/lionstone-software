## Overview

*Pridefinder* is a focus-timer app for people who bounce off productivity tools that punish you for missing a day. Each focus session draws a new resident to your sanctuary; skipped days don't reset anything.

## Highlights

- **No streaks.** Progress accumulates; it never resets.
- **Local-first.** Your data lives on your device. Optional encrypted sync.
- **Beautiful, calm.** Hand-illustrated animals, soft palette, minimal sound.

## Status

Currently in **closed beta** on Android. Web companion view is read-only for now; full web app is on the roadmap.
